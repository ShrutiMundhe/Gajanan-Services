Furniture Care Specialists

Extend Your Furniture's Life

Give your furniture the professional care it deserves. Our specialized furniture cleaning service protects and refreshes wooden, metal, and fabric furniture — removing dust, stains, odors, and allergens carefully without causing any damage. We understand that different materials require specific treatment approaches and use appropriate cleaning methods for each furniture type.

From antique wooden pieces to modern upholstery, from office chairs to luxury sofas — we restore your furniture to its original beauty while extending its lifespan and maintaining its value.

Furniture Types

Specialized Care Methods

Expert cleaning techniques tailored to each furniture material and type.

Wooden Furniture

Gentle dusting, polishing, and conditioning to maintain wood's natural beauty and prevent cracking or fading.

Upholstered & Fabric

Deep cleaning for sofas, chairs, and cushions using steam cleaning and safe fabric-specific treatments.

Metal & Plastic

Thorough cleaning and protective treatment for metal chairs, tables, and modern plastic furniture.

Office Furniture

Professional cleaning for office chairs, desks, filing cabinets, and ergonomic furniture maintenance.
