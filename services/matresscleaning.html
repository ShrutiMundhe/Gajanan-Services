FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Mattress deep cleaning includes thorough dry vacuuming to remove surface dust, dead skin cells, and loose allergens. We then apply fabric-safe shampoo, perform mechanized scrub-cleaning, extract deep-seated moisture and dirt using a high-suction machine, and sanitize the mattress to remove bacteria and odors.
Typically, a mattress takes about 4 to 8 hours to dry completely depending on room temperature, ventilation, and air circulation. We recommend leaving the ceiling fan running and windows open during this time.
We treat all stains with professional stain removers. Most fresh sweat, food, and beverage stains will be significantly reduced or completely removed. However, old hard-water stains, ink, or chemical discolorations might only fade as the fabric fibers might have been permanently dyed.
Mattresses accumulate dead skin flakes, sweat, dust mites, and bacteria over time. Professional cleaning sanitizes the fabric, removes these allergens, improves bedroom air quality, and ensures a healthier sleep environment.
