Our Story

Committed to Clean Spaces Since Day One

Gajanan Cleaning Services was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to deliver professional, reliable, and affordable cleaning services to every home and business in Pune.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellence, punctuality, and thoroughness. Our trained team of professionals brings expertise, dedication, and the latest cleaning equipment to every job — no matter how big or small.

We specialize in Home Cleaning, Office Cleaning, Floor Deep Cleaning, Sofa & Carpet Cleaning, Glass & Exterior Cleaning, Sanitization & Disinfection, Post-Construction Cleaning, Water Tank Cleaning, and much more.
